# Our Vision

DETI MakerLab (DML) is a system designed to manage a modern and innovative room. This room is filled with electronic components and devices, such as Arduinos, Raspberries, 3D printers, a network closet and the like. The space aims at being the room to carry on projects inside DETI.

Our goal is to provide a flexible platform to manage an hacker space like DML, easing the students’ and manager’s lives, and to solve some of the space current issues.
